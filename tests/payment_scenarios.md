# Telegram Stars Payment Scenarios\n\n## Scope\nThis checklist validates the Stars-only flow end-to-end:\n- product listing\n- invoice payment\n- purchase completion\n- auto delivery of signed download link\n\n## Scenario 1: Successful purchase\n1. Open bot and run /start.\n2. Open product list and choose a pack.\n3. Tap Buy with Stars.\n4. Complete Telegram Stars payment.\n5. Verify user receives message with 24h download URL.\n6. Verify purchase status becomes completed in admin panel.\n7. Verify stars_total increases by pack price.\n\nExpected result:\n- Purchase moves from pending to completed automatically.\n- telegram_payment_charge_id is stored.\n- Sold counter for product increases by 1.\n\n## Scenario 2: Invalid payload safety\n1. Simulate successful_payment update with malformed payload.\n2. Check bot behavior.\n\nExpected result:\n- Bot does not crash.\n- No unrelated purchase is updated.\n\n## Scenario 3: Duplicate callback safety\n1. Send same successful_payment callback twice (same payload).\n2. Inspect purchase and sold counters.\n\nExpected result:\n- Purchase remains completed.\n- Sold counter should not double-increment for same purchase.\n\n## Scenario 4: Missing S3 object\n1. Mark payment successful for a product with missing zip object.\n2. Observe bot/admin behavior.\n\nExpected result:\n- System logs delivery failure safely.\n- Purchase record remains auditable.\n- No unhandled exception terminates bot process.\n\n## Scenario 5: Mini App purchase visibility\n1. Open Mini App from Telegram bot.\n2. Navigate to My purchases.\n3. Confirm latest purchase appears with status and charge id.\n\nExpected result:\n- User sees only own purchases.\n- Unauthorized access without valid initData is blocked.\n\n## Scenario 6: Manual admin confirm fallback\n1. Create pending purchase manually in DB/admin route.\n2. Confirm purchase from panel endpoint.\n\nExpected result:\n- Purchase becomes completed.\n- User receives 24h signed URL.\n
